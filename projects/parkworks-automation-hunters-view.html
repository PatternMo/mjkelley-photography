Project Overview

This project showcases the innovative automated parking systems installed by Parkworks Mechanical Systems at the Hunters View residential development in San Francisco. The objective was to artfully and realistically portray these cutting-edge automated parking systems within modern residential parking garages that are part of new residential building developments in the Bay Area.

Through careful composition and expert lighting, these images capture both the technological sophistication and seamless integration of automated parking solutions in contemporary urban residential architecture.

  • Client: Parkworks Mechanical Systems
  • Location: Hunters View, San Francisco
  • Project Type: Automated Parking Systems
  • Building Type: Modern Residential Development
  • Photography Focus: Architectural & Systems Documentation
  • Coverage: Interior parking garage systems and installations

This comprehensive documentation serves to showcase the advanced engineering and clean integration of automated parking technology in urban residential environments.

Creative Process

Pre-Production Planning
Site assessment and coordination with Parkworks Mechanical Systems to understand the automated parking system functionality and optimal shooting angles. Planning for lighting conditions within the parking garage environment and identifying key features to highlight.
Photography Session
On-site documentation capturing the automated parking system from multiple perspectives, emphasizing both the technological components and their integration within the modern residential architecture. Focus on clean compositions that showcase the engineering precision and space efficiency.
Lighting & Composition
Strategic lighting setup to illuminate the parking system's mechanical components and structural elements while maintaining the authentic garage environment. Careful attention to lines, angles, and depth to create visually compelling representations of the automated technology.
Post-Production
Professional editing and color correction to ensure consistency across all images while maintaining realistic representation of the automated parking systems. Enhancement of architectural details and mechanical components without compromising authenticity.
Final Delivery
High-resolution images optimized for marketing materials, presentations, and portfolio use. All files delivered with usage rights for Parkworks Mechanical Systems' promotional and documentation purposes.
