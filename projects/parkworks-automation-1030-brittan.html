Project Overview

This project showcases the innovative automated parking systems installed by Parkworks Mechanical Systems at the 1030 Brittan residential development in San Carlos. The objective was to artfully and realistically portray these cutting-edge automated parking systems within modern residential parking garages that are part of new residential building developments in the Bay Area.

A distinctive feature of this installation is the internal lighting system for the parking area, which offers a cool light temperature that was utilized to highlight the mechanical structures and parking pallets. For the wider views outside the system, natural afternoon light poured in from the garage's entrance and exit doors, creating dynamic contrast between the industrial precision of the automated system and the architectural context. This garage also features valets to assist residents with the parking system.

  • Client: Parkworks Mechanical Systems
  • Location: 1030 Brittan, San Carlos
  • Project Type: Automated Parking Systems
  • Building Type: Modern Residential Development
  • Photography Focus: Architectural & Systems Documentation
  • Coverage: Interior parking garage systems and installations
  • Special Feature: Valet-assisted automated parking system

This comprehensive documentation serves to showcase the advanced engineering and clean integration of automated parking technology in urban residential environments.

Creative Process

Pre-Production Planning
Site assessment and coordination with Parkworks Mechanical Systems to understand the automated parking system functionality and optimal shooting angles. Planning for lighting conditions within the parking garage environment and identifying key features to highlight.
Photography Session
On-site documentation capturing the automated parking system from multiple perspectives, emphasizing both the technological components and their integration within the modern residential architecture. Focus on clean compositions that showcase the engineering precision and space efficiency.
Lighting & Composition
The facility's internal lighting system provided a cool light temperature that was strategically utilized to highlight the mechanical structures and parking pallets, emphasizing their industrial precision. For wider contextual views, natural afternoon light streaming through the garage's entrance and exit doors created dynamic interplay between the automated system's technical details and the broader architectural environment. Careful attention to lines, angles, and depth created visually compelling representations of the technology.
Post-Production
Professional editing and color correction to ensure consistency across all images while maintaining realistic representation of the automated parking systems. Enhancement of architectural details and mechanical components without compromising authenticity.
Final Delivery
High-resolution images optimized for marketing materials, presentations, and portfolio use. All files delivered with usage rights for Parkworks Mechanical Systems' promotional and documentation purposes.
