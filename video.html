Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a video production package?
Each package includes pre-production planning, professional filming with cinema-grade equipment, drone footage (when applicable), color grading, audio enhancement, and final edited video delivery in your preferred format.
How long does video production take?
Production timelines vary by project scope. Simple promotional videos typically take 1-2 weeks from shoot to delivery, while complex documentaries or commercials may require 3-6 weeks for completion.
Do you provide drone services for all projects?
As a Part 107 certified pilot, I can provide drone footage for most projects. However, flight restrictions apply in certain areas. I'll verify airspace permissions and obtain necessary authorizations for your location.
What video formats do you deliver?
I deliver videos optimized for your intended use - web streaming, social media, broadcast, or cinema. Common formats include MP4 for web, ProRes for broadcast, and custom specifications for specific platforms.
Can you handle both filming and post-production?
Yes, I provide complete video production services from concept to final delivery, including cinematography, drone operations, editing, color correction, audio mixing, and motion graphics when needed.
Do you travel outside the Bay Area?
While based in Berkeley, I'm available for projects throughout California and beyond. Travel fees apply for locations outside the Bay Area, and I can coordinate multi-day shoots for destination projects.
