Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in an architectural photography session?
Each session includes professional lighting setup, multiple angles and compositions, basic post-processing, and high-resolution digital files delivered within 48-72 hours. Drone photography can be added for exterior shots when permitted.
How long does a typical shoot take?
Residential properties typically require 2-4 hours, while larger commercial spaces may need 4-8 hours. The timeline depends on the size of the property, number of spaces, and specific requirements.
Do you provide staging advice?
Yes, I provide guidance on preparing spaces for photography, including lighting, decluttering, and styling tips to ensure your property looks its absolute best.
What areas do you serve?
I serve the entire San Francisco Bay Area, including San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, San Jose, Palo Alto, and surrounding communities. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond 30 miles from Berkeley.
Can you photograph occupied properties?
Absolutely. I work efficiently and respectfully in occupied spaces, coordinating with tenants and property managers to minimize disruption while capturing beautiful images.
