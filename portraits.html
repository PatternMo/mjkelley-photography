Creative Portrait Photography Services

Specializing in portrait photography that captures the essence of who you are. My approach combines artistic vision with personal connection to create images that tell your unique story and showcase your authentic personality.

Working throughout the San Francisco Bay Area, I provide comprehensive portrait photography services for:

  • Individual creative portraits
  • Yoga portrait sessions
  • Lifestyle and environmental portraits
  • Milestone and celebration photography
  • Personal branding portraits
  • Artistic and fine art portraiture

Each session is designed to make you feel comfortable and confident while creating beautiful, timeless images. Whether in our Berkeley studio or at stunning Bay Area locations, I ensure every portrait reflects your personality and the relationships that matter most.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a portrait photography session?
Each session includes professional lighting setup, multiple poses and compositions, outfit changes if desired, basic post-processing, and high-resolution digital files delivered within 7-10 days. Sessions can be in my Berkeley studio or on location.
How long does a portrait session take?
Most portrait sessions last 1-2 hours, allowing time for multiple outfit changes, different poses, and various lighting setups.
Do you photograph families and groups?
No, I specialize in individual portraits for professionals, yogis, actors, and models.
What should we wear for our portrait session?
The key is choosing clothes that make you feel confident and reflect your personality.
Can we choose between studio and outdoor locations?
Absolutely! I offer both studio sessions in Berkeley and outdoor sessions at beautiful Bay Area locations. We can discuss the best option based on your style preferences, needs, and the type of portraits you envision.
How many final images will we receive?
Most sessions include 2-20 professionally edited final images in high resolution based on your needs. The exact number depends on session length and type. Additional images can be purchased, and I'm happy to customize packages based on your specific needs.
