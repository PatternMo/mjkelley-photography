Project Overview

This project exemplifies the comprehensive approach to visual storytelling that combines both photography and videography to create a complete narrative. The work showcases the subject from multiple perspectives, capturing both intimate details and expansive views.

Working with both ground-level and aerial photography, this project demonstrates the full range of creative possibilities when documenting a subject. Each image and video frame was carefully composed to contribute to the overall story.

  • Photography: Canon 5D Mark IV with 24-70mm f/2.8
  • Drone: DJI Mavic 3 Pro with 4K video capability
  • Duration: Full day shoot with golden hour timing
  • Deliverables: 50+ edited photos and 3-minute video
  • Location: San Francisco Bay Area
  • Post-production: Adobe Lightroom and Premiere Pro

The combination of still photography and video allows for a richer storytelling experience, providing clients with versatile content for various marketing and presentation needs.

Creative Process

Pre-Production Planning
Comprehensive site visit and planning session to understand the subject, lighting conditions, and optimal timing. Flight planning for drone operations and equipment selection based on project requirements.
Photography Session
Ground-level photography session capturing multiple angles, compositions, and lighting scenarios. Focus on both wide establishing shots and detailed close-ups to tell the complete visual story.
Aerial Videography
Professional drone operations to capture aerial perspectives and smooth cinematic video sequences. All flights conducted under Part 107 certification with proper safety protocols.
Post-Production
Professional editing and color grading to ensure consistency across all deliverables. Video editing includes music selection, pacing, and seamless transitions between aerial and ground footage.
Final Delivery
High-resolution images optimized for various uses, plus professionally edited video in multiple formats. All files delivered with usage rights and organized for easy access and distribution.
