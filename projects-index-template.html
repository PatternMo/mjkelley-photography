Project Frequently Asked Questions

How do you structure multi-day projects?
Large projects are broken into phases with detailed planning, shot lists, and coordination schedules. I provide project timelines, regular check-ins, and preliminary image reviews to ensure we stay on track and meet all objectives.
What is included in project deliverables?
Complete projects include planning documentation, raw and edited high-resolution images, web-optimized versions, usage rights documentation, and organized delivery via cloud storage with clear file naming conventions.
Can you coordinate with other vendors?
Yes, I regularly collaborate with marketing teams, designers, writers, videographers, and event coordinators to ensure seamless project execution and consistent creative vision across all deliverables.
How do you handle project revisions?
Each project includes two rounds of revisions on selected images. Additional editing or reshooting can be accommodated within the project scope or as separate line items, depending on the request.
What usage rights come with project work?
Usage rights are tailored to each project's needs. Standard packages include broad commercial use for marketing and promotion. Extended rights for resale, licensing, or exclusive use are available with appropriate licensing agreements.
Do you provide project case studies?
With client approval, I document project processes and results as case studies for both portfolio use and client reference. These help demonstrate methodology and can serve as valuable marketing materials for your organization.
