Professional Automotive Photography Services

Specializing in automotive photography that showcases vehicles with dramatic lighting, dynamic compositions, and meticulous attention to detail. My approach combines technical precision with artistic vision to create images that capture the essence and character of each vehicle.

Working throughout the San Francisco Bay Area, I provide comprehensive automotive photography services for:

  • Car dealerships and sales lots
  • Classic car collectors and enthusiasts
  • Automotive brands and manufacturers
  • Racing teams and events
  • Motorcycle dealers and custom builders
  • Auto shows and exhibitions

Each shoot is carefully planned to highlight the unique design elements and craftsmanship that make your vehicle special. From dramatic hero shots to detailed interior and engine bay photography, I ensure every image reflects the quality and passion behind automotive excellence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in an automotive photography session?
Each session includes professional lighting setup, multiple angles and compositions, exterior and interior shots, detail photography, basic post-processing, and high-resolution digital files delivered within 48-72 hours. Additional services like drone photography or lifestyle shots can be added.
How long does a typical automotive shoot take?
A single vehicle session typically requires 1-2 hours for comprehensive coverage, while dealership inventory shoots depend on the number of vehicles. Multiple vehicle sessions and custom collections may require 3-6 hours.
Do you provide location scouting?
Yes, I can recommend and scout locations that complement your vehicle's character. From urban settings and scenic coastal roads to studio environments, I help select the perfect backdrop for your automotive photography.
Can you photograph vehicles in motion?
Absolutely. I specialize in dynamic automotive photography including rolling shots, panning techniques, and action photography that captures vehicles in motion while maintaining sharp focus and dramatic effect.
Do you work with dealerships for inventory photography?
Yes, I provide efficient inventory photography services for dealerships throughout the Bay Area. I can work on-site at your location with standardized lighting and angles to ensure consistent, professional images for your entire inventory.
