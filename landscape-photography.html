Fine Art Landscape & Aerial Photography

Exploring California's diverse landscapes through the lens, from the rolling hills of the Bay Area to the dramatic peaks of the Sierra Nevada. My work captures the raw beauty and quiet majesty of the natural world.

As a Part 107 certified drone pilot, I combine traditional landscape photography with aerial perspectives to create unique visual narratives. Services include:

  • Fine art landscape prints
  • Commercial licensing for publications
  • Aerial photography and video
  • Nature and wildlife photography
  • Custom landscape commissions
  • Stock photography licensing

Each image is carefully composed to showcase the natural drama and beauty of California's wilderness. From intimate forest details to sweeping vistas, my goal is to create images that transport viewers into these remarkable landscapes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you sell fine art prints of your landscape work?
Yes, high-quality fine art prints are available in various sizes and materials including metal, canvas, and archival paper. Each print is professionally produced and can be custom framed. Contact me for current print availability and pricing.
Where do you typically photograph landscapes?
I photograph throughout California, with regular trips to the Sierra Nevada, Big Sur coastline, Marin Headlands, Point Reyes, and various national and state parks. I'm always exploring new locations and happy to travel for commissioned work.
Can you provide aerial photography for commercial projects?
Absolutely. As a Part 107 certified drone pilot, I provide commercial aerial photography and videography services for real estate, tourism, environmental documentation, and marketing projects throughout California.
Do you offer licensing for commercial use?
Yes, I license images for commercial use including publications, websites, marketing materials, and editorial projects. Rights-managed and royalty-free options are available depending on your needs.
Do you accept custom landscape photography commissions?
I welcome custom commissions for specific locations or projects. Whether you need images of a particular area, seasonal captures, or aerial perspectives of your property, I can work with you to create exactly what you envision.
