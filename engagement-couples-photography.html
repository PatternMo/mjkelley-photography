Engagement & Couples Photography

Capturing authentic moments and celebrating your connection. Romantic, surprise engagements and couples' sessions throughout the San Francisco Bay Area.

Authentic Love Story Photography

Every couple has a unique story to tell. My approach to engagement and couples photography focuses on capturing the genuine emotions, spontaneous laughter, and intimate moments that define your relationship.

From romantic sunset sessions to playful urban adventures, I create a relaxed environment where your authentic connection can shine through. Each session is tailored to reflect your personalities and style:

  • Engagement sessions for wedding announcements
  • Anniversary and milestone celebrations
  • Couples portraits for holiday cards
  • Save-the-date photography
  • Maternity couples sessions
  • Romantic getaway documentation

Whether you envision a dreamy beach session at sunset, an urban adventure through San Francisco, or an intimate garden setting, I'll work with you to create images that authentically represent your love story and connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should we expect during our engagement session?
Your session will be relaxed and fun! I'll guide you through natural poses and interactions while capturing candid moments. Sessions typically last 1-2 hours and include multiple outdoor scenes in one location. I'll help you feel comfortable and natural in front of the camera.
Where can we have our session?
The Bay Area offers incredible locations! Popular spots include Baker Beach, Crissy Field, Golden Gate Park, Muir Woods, downtown San Francisco, Berkeley Rose Garden, or any location that's meaningful to your relationship. I'm happy to suggest locations based on your style preferences.
What should we wear for our photos?
Wear something that makes you feel confident and comfortable! I recommend coordinating colors rather than matching exactly, avoid busy patterns, and bring layers.
How many photos will we receive?
You'll receive 15-20 professionally edited high-resolution images from a typical session, delivered through a private online gallery within 7-14 days. All images are ready for printing and sharing on social media.
Can you photograph our proposal?
Absolutely! Proposal photography is one of my favorite services. I'll work discreetly to capture the surprise and emotion of the moment, then continue with an impromptu engagement session if you'd like. Advanced planning and coordination are essential for successful proposal shoots.
What's the best time of day for our session?
The golden hour (one hour before sunset) provides the most flattering light for romantic photos. However, I can work with any time that fits your schedule. Morning sessions offer soft light and fewer crowds, while midday sessions work well for urban or shaded locations.
