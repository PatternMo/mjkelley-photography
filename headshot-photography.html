Professional Headshot & Portrait Services

Specializing in headshot and portrait photography that showcases your unique personality and professional brand. My approach combines technical expertise with an understanding of how to make you feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my headshot session?
Choose clothing that reflects your professional image and makes you feel confident. Solid colors work best, avoiding busy patterns or bright whites.
How long does a headshot session take?
Individual headshot sessions typically last 30-60 minutes, allowing time for multiple outfit changes and various looks. Group sessions and corporate team shoots are scheduled and priced based on the number of participants.
Do you provide hair and makeup services?
I work with talented hair and makeup artists who can be hired separately. Professional grooming can enhance your overall look and boost confidence during the shoot.
When will I receive my photos?
You'll receive a gallery of professionally edited high-resolution images within 7-10 days from the day you make your final selections. The gallery includes high-resolution, edited versions of your selections.
Can we shoot outdoors or only in the studio?
I offer studio sessions in my Berkeley location and on-location office and outdoor sessions throughout the Bay Area. Outdoor sessions can provide beautiful natural light and scenic backgrounds that complement your professional image. For office sessions, I require a large space where I can setup a temporary studio to capture your team.
How many final images do I receive?
Most sessions include 1-2 professionally edited final images in high resolution per person. Additional images can be purchased, and I'm happy to customize packages based on your specific needs.
