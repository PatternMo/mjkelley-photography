Professional Headshot & Portrait Services

Specializing in headshot and portrait photography that showcases your unique personality and professional brand. My approach combines technical expertise with an understanding of how to make you feel comfortable and confident in front of the camera.

Working throughout the San Francisco Bay Area from my Berkeley studio and on location, I provide comprehensive portrait services for:

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for my headshot session?
Choose clothing that reflects your professional image and makes you feel confident. Solid colors work best, avoiding busy patterns or bright whites. I provide a detailed style guide upon booking to help you prepare for your session.
How long does a headshot session take?
Individual headshot sessions typically last 1-2 hours, allowing time for multiple outfit changes and various looks. Group sessions and corporate team shoots are scheduled based on the number of participants.
Do you provide hair and makeup services?
I work with talented hair and makeup artists who can be added to your session for an additional fee. Professional grooming can enhance your overall look and boost confidence during the shoot.
When will I receive my photos?
You'll receive a gallery of professionally edited high-resolution images within 48-72 hours of your session. The gallery includes both color and black & white versions of your selected images.
Can we shoot outdoors or only in the studio?
I offer both studio sessions in my Berkeley location and outdoor sessions throughout the Bay Area. Outdoor sessions can provide beautiful natural light and scenic backgrounds that complement your professional image.
How many final images do I receive?
Most sessions include 10-15 professionally edited final images in high resolution. Additional images can be purchased, and I'm happy to customize packages based on your specific needs.
