Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in a real estate photography session?
Each session includes professional lighting setup, multiple angles and compositions, exterior and interior shots, basic post-processing, and high-resolution digital files delivered within 7-10 days. Drone photography, video, and twilight shots can be added when conditions permit.
How long does a real estate photography shoot take?
Most residential properties require 1-3 hours depending on size and number of rooms. Larger luxury properties or commercial spaces may need 3-6 hours for complete coverage.
Do you provide same-day or next-day delivery?
Yes, I understand the fast-paced nature of real estate. Standard delivery is within 7-10 days, with same-day and rush delivery available for an additional fee when booked in advance.
What preparation is needed before the shoot?
I provide a detailed preparation checklist including decluttering, lighting optimization, and staging suggestions. Proper preparation ensures the property photographs beautifully and appeals to the maximum number of potential buyers.
Do you offer twilight and drone photography?
Yes, twilight photography showcases properties with warm, inviting lighting, while drone photography provides stunning aerial perspectives. Both services can be added to create premium marketing packages that help listings stand out.
What areas do you serve in the Bay Area?
I serve the entire San Francisco Bay Area including San Francisco, Oakland, Berkeley, San Jose, Palo Alto, Marin County, and surrounding communities. Travel fees may apply for locations beyond 30 miles from Berkeley.
